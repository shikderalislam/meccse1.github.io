About Us

Mymensingh Engineering College (Bengali: ময়মনসিংহ প্রকৌশল মহাবিদ্যালয়) commonly referred to as MEC (Bengali: মইক) is a public undergraduate college in Mymensingh, Bangladesh, established in 2007. MEC is an elite institution for graduating in engineering disciplines in Bangladesh, and is affiliated with the University of Dhaka under the Faculty of Engineering & Technology. It is the modern engineering colleges in Bangladesh. Postgraduate studies and more Engineering departments are going to be introduced soon. Mymensingh Engineering College is located about 7 kilometers away from the main city of Mymensingh.
